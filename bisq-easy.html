Bisq Easy

Bisq Easy was launched in March 2024 as the first Bisq 2 trade protocol, designed especially for new and novice bitcoin buyers to get their first bitcoin in a safe, private and self-custodial way.



Getting started with Bisq Easy:

  1. Get Bisq 2 from the downloads page
  2. Explore the Bisq Easy offerbook or create your own offer
  3. Share your feedback in the integrated p2p group chat

Find out more about Bisq Easy in the Bisq Wiki.
Learn about the Bisq 2 roadmap at the Bisq 2 launch blog post.

Note: Already running Bisq 1? No problem! Bisq 2 is a separate application and will install and run alongside Bisq 1 without issue.
